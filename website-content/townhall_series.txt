Dear Executive Director
I am writing on behalf of the Viable Alternative Republic (VAR 37/38), in partnership with Uamuzi (civic
technology) and the National Economic Development Party (NEDP) Youth League, to introduce Democracy
Activated, a strategic youth civic mobilization program designed to convert Kenya’s youthful protest and digital
energy into structured, sustained democratic participation.
We have attached the full project document titled “DEMOCRACY ACTIVATED: VAR YOUTH TOWNHALL SERIES :
From the Streets to the Ballot”. The document outlines a well-structured pilot to be implemented in Trans Nzoia
County as a proof of concept for national scaling.
Key Highlights of the Initiative
Theme: Democracy Activated | Ideology Meets Technology | From the Streets to the Ballot
Target: Over 1,000 young people in a high-impact county townhall
Model: Three integrated pillars – Streets (Connect) led by VAR, Ideology (Converse) led by NEDP Youth
League, and Technology (Continue) powered by the Uamuzi platform
Core Objective: Bridge the gap between youth street activism and formal democratic processes (voter
registration, issue-based politics, accountable leadership, and sustained civic networks)
Pilot Budget: KES 5,000,000 (detailed breakdown included in the document)
Trans Nzoia presents an ideal pilot location: agriculturally strategic, youthful, and politically diverse, yet with
notable gaps in structured youth engagement. This intervention will deliver measurable outcomes: a county-level
youth civic network, increased voter registration commitments, digital onboarding for continuity, youth
champions, and rich content to support VAR’s national narrative.
Given CMD-Kenya’s longstanding leadership in strengthening multiparty democracy, civic education, political
party development, and meaningful youth inclusion in governance, we believe this initiative aligns strongly with
your organization’s mission and strategic priorities.
A successful partnership with CMD-Kenya would add significant credibility, technical depth, and potential for
wider reach and sustainability.
We are therefore requesting your support in the following possible forms:
1. Financial support for the KES 5 million pilot (full or partial)
2. Technical and strategic partnership (co-hosting, civic education content, monitoring & evaluation)
3. Endorsement and institutional backing to enhance legitimacy and mobilization
We would be delighted to present the full proposal in detail and explore how we can work together to make this
pilot a model for youth democratic activation across Kenya. Please let us know a convenient time.
Thank you for your continued leadership in Kenya’s democratic journey. We look forward to the possibility of
partnering with CMD-Kenya on this timely and impactful moment
