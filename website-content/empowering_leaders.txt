Who We Are: VAR 37-38
VAR is a dynamic, youth-led civic movement built on transformative
action. VAR 37-38 represents empowered youth utilizing constitutional
rights to assemble (Art. 37) and channel energy into political action (Art.
38).
We represent a national network of digitally-savvy, grassroots-connected
young Kenyans actively shaping a more participatory, accountable, and
peaceful political future.
Our core value: an intrinsic, trusted connection to Kenya's youth
demographic4over 75% of the population4engaging them in their own
spaces and cultural contexts.
